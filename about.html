Our story

About AOCO Locksmiths LLC

Our mission is to provide Irving residents and businesses with fast, reliable, and affordable locksmith services. We are committed to excellence and customer satisfaction.

AOCO Locksmiths LLC was founded with the goal of bringing trustworthy locksmith services to the Irving community. With over 15 years of combined experience, our team has the expertise to handle any lock or security challenge. We started small, focusing on residential calls, and grew through word-of-mouth referrals by consistently delivering quality work and fair prices.

Today, we serve homeowners, businesses, and car owners throughout the Dallas-Fort Worth metroplex. Our technicians are trained on the latest security technologies and carry a wide range of parts to ensure fast, on-the-spot solutions. We pride ourselves on being a local, family-owned business that treats every customer like a neighbor.
